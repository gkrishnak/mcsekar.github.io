About the Company

SchoolCreative Institute of the Arts prepares students for rewarding careers in the creative sector. Students are able to hone their talent and business IQ with comprehensive programs in acting, digital photography, animation, screenwriting, video game programming and design. Located in Vancouver, B.C., the school provides its students with the opportunity to learn and network in a city that caters to the arts.
